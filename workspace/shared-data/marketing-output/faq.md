# DPDP Act 2023 FAQ

**Q: What does 'Consent Requirement' require?**
A: No personal data processing without consent. (Section 4(1))

**Q: What does 'Purpose Limitation' require?**
A: Data shall not be processed for purposes other than consent. (Section 5(1))

**Q: What does 'Notice Requirements' require?**
A: Must provide notice before collecting consent. (Section 6)

**Q: What does 'Children's Data' require?**
A: Verifiable parental consent required. (Section 8)

**Q: What does 'Breach Notification' require?**
A: Must notify Board and individuals of breaches. (Section 10)

# Regulation Summary (STUB for M0 testing — DPDP Act 2023 + Rules 2025)

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, governs the processing of digital
personal data. It gives Data Principals (individuals) rights over their data and imposes
obligations on Data Fiduciaries (organisations that decide how/why data is processed).
The DPDP Rules, 2025 specify operational security safeguards.

Scope for this stub: the engineering-relevant obligations only. See obligations.json.
